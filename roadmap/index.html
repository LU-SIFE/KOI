WEBKOI Roadmap


Please keep in mind, not all features may make an update, they may be pushed to another update down the line, or changed altogether.

V1.1.0

Major Content Update!

End of July

This will be the first major update of WEBKOI, focusing on adding content. There's a lot of unused ideas and features that exist in the game already, and this update will focus on bringing WEBKOI together.


Features:


  • Market! Complete fish bounties, buy items, and check the current weather patterns.
  • Items! Find/buy items to help you on your fishing journey.

  • Chests and Keys! Fish up precious treasure, as well as the keys to open them up.
  • Who knows what might be lost down in the pond...

  • Fishdex! with so many fish, why not start collecting some?
  • Did someone say collection? Like, shiny fish?

  • More Fish! I plan to push the total number of unique fish to 200.
  • With so many fish, we need a "Sell All" button... (Added in v1.0.0a)

  • More Ponds! There's a bunch of unused fish types, and new ponds will make them accessible!
  • we really need a pond selection menu... (Added in v1.0.0a)

  • More Upgrades! I know 3 upgrades isn't all that much, so more will be added.
  • Lost journals, lore, and more! (in my fishing game??)

  • And...
  • Cosmetics!!! Player outlines, autofisher colors, ripple effects, pond effects, you name it!

  • Seasons! catch special fish based on the current season.
  • Day/Night Cycle! certain fish may only appear late at night...

    • Stay tuned for more updates, and thank you for supporting WEBKOI!
